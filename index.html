I am an incoming PhD student at the University of Toronto with the Connaught International Scholarship, advised by Professor Zhijing Jin. I will also be associated with the Vector Institute. Previously, I finished my MS degree advised by Se-Young Yun and also collaborated closely with Professor Kimin Lee. I finished my undergraduate degree at Seoul National University, with my last semester collaborating with Taesup Kim.

My research focuses on enabling AI models to perform real-world tasks while being safe and reliable. Currently, I am primarily interested in open-endedness, multi-agent systems, alignment, and AI safety. However, I'm open to exploring any interesting problems in the field.
